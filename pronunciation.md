# Pronunciation


## Vowel Sound

### `/ɪ/`

short vowel sound

#### Words

| word   | pronounce |
|:-------|:----------|
| kit    | /kɪt/     |
| bid    | /bɪd/     |
| hymn   | /hɪm/     |
| minute | /ˈmɪnɪt/  |

#### Similar Words

`/ɪ/` short vowel <br/>
`/iː/` long vowel

| word | pronounce | -   | word  | pronounce |
|:-----|:----------|:----|:------|:----------|
| hit  | /hɪt/     | -   | heat  | /hiːt/    |
| lip  | /lɪp/     | -   | leap  | /liːp/    |
| sick | /sɪk/     | -   | seek  | /siːk/    |
| will | /wɪl/     | -   | wheel | /wiːl/    |
